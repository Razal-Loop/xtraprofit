Net Profit Calculation

Calculated as (Selling Price - Cost Price). This represents the raw liquidity realized from an asset disposal prior to taxation and custodial fees.

Margin Optimization

Margin is derived via (Net Profit / Selling Price). Unlike markup, margin accounts for the total percentage of the revenue that is retained as surplus.

Discount Calibration

Sophisticated investors use fractional discounts to simulate early-entry incentives or large-lot liquidity adjustments.
